About

Daniel is a Canadian Business Consultant to Local & International clients, specializing in Digital Marketing in the Mobile Web Space. He develops custom Optimization solutions for Search & Social Media Marketing, Networking & Management.

He has worked closely with developers from Google, Microsoft, Facebook, WordPress, and several other start-ups & Fortune 500 companies, Daniel wears an ethical White Hat and is currently the Senior Strategist of Revolutionary End-to-End Innovation at Profound Ideation Inc.

Daniel is more than a Full-Stack Front & Back-End Website Developer, WordPress Expert, HolographicSEO Visionary and Serial Entrepreneur, he is also a Ruby on Rails Programmer, who is currently working on projects involving AngularJS, NodeJS, Heroku, Git, Cyber-Security, Mobile & Platform Games Development and Lead Generation. Daniel's next conquest will be in relation to ReactJS, Big Data, Data Mining, the Internet of Things, including Parallel Processing and Growth Hacking.
